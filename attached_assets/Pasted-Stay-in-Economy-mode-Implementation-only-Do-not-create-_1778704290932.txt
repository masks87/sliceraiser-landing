Page title:
How It Works

Page subtitle:
A simple journey to explore property, equity and fixed income opportunities through SliceRaiser.

Intro text:
SliceRaiser is designed to make investment access clearer, more structured and easier to understand. From exploring opportunities to tracking your dashboard, the platform gives users one digital path to discover selected investment opportunities and become part of the SliceRaiser investment lifestyle.

Step 1 title:
Explore Opportunities

Step 1 text:
Browse selected opportunities across property, equity and fixed income. Each opportunity is presented with key information to help users understand the category, structure, market and available details before taking the next step.

Step 2 title:
Create Your Account

Step 2 text:
Create a SliceRaiser account to access a more complete platform experience, save opportunities and begin your investment journey through one secure digital platform.

Step 3 title:
Complete Onboarding

Step 3 text:
Users may be asked to complete onboarding steps such as identity details, contact information and required declarations before participating in available opportunities.

Step 4 title:
Review the Opportunity

Step 4 text:
Before making any decision, users can review the opportunity details, documents, risks, timeline and investment structure. SliceRaiser is built to make this information easier to access and understand.

Step 5 title:
Start from USD 100

Step 5 text:
SliceRaiser is designed to make investment participation more accessible, with opportunities starting from USD 100 where available.

Step 6 title:
Track Your Dashboard

Step 6 text:
After participation, users can track activity through their dashboard, including investment status, updates, documents, portfolio overview and distributions where applicable.

Step 7 title:
Receive Updates

Step 7 text:
SliceRaiser aims to keep users informed with updates on opportunity progress, project milestones, reporting and relevant platform notifications.

Step 8 title:
Continue or Exit

Step 8 text:
Depending on the structure of each opportunity, users may continue holding, receive distributions where applicable or follow the exit process described in the opportunity documents.

Final section title:
Built for the SliceRaiser Investment Lifestyle

Final section text:
SliceRaiser is more than a platform to browse opportunities. It is a modern investment lifestyle built around access, participation, ownership mindset and long term value creation.
